CASE STUDY

THE EMIRATES FA CUP

How we maximised engagement for fans at the 2016 FA Cup

PHASE 01

awareness

The FA Cup Semi-Finals have concluded,
there are 28 days left until the final.

  • 02
  • 03
  • 04

  1. OUTDOOR SCREEN
    MESSAGING

    We designed congratulatory messages to the two winning teams distributed across Wembley Stadium’s large external screen.
    Our objective is to create content that educates fans across social media from the moment they leave the stadium.

  2. SOCIAL MEDIA ENGAGEMENT

    Social media is utilised between 28–14 days before an event to inform fans about the up and coming final. Such details include, a re-cap of the semi-final; the date and kick off time of the final and any up and coming ticket news for their respective club.

  6. COUNTDOWN
    SCREENS

    It is at this point, 14 days before an event that we begin to increase the anticipation for the cup final. We focus on clear communication messages such as a promotional messaging for the FA Cup and an official countdown to the final itself.

  7. FACEBOOK CANVAS

    Social media is used to build excitement and helps us to educate fans on any last minute information, such as, your club’s travel arrangements; where to grab a drink before the match and where you’ll be situated within the ground.

  8. INSTAGRAM COMPETITION

    Giveaways are a good way to gain social media attention in the build-up to an event.

  9. BUILD UP
    MICROSITE

    We can also utilise social media to help aggregate fan’s own user generated content via a dedicated microsite. This content on this page can then be used and distributed across both social media and digital screens on event day to encourage further engagement both online and in and around the stadium.

  11. EVENT DAY
    SCREENS

    From the moment a fan arrives on Wembley way to finding their seat within the stadium, it’s our objective to deliver digital screen communications that entertain, educate and excite fans attending the FA Cup Final

  12. MATCH REPORT WEBSITE

    We produce bespoke content for Club Wembley, giving fans access to an online breakdown of the event.

  13. SOCIAL MEDIA
    RECAP

    Social media is utilised to summarise the match’s key moments following the event. We create bespoke content that allows fans to relive the momentous moments from the occasion, such as goal celebrations, game statistics, including the man of the match and the successful team picking up the FA Cup.
